[stack manipulation][push][0][ * 2 + 1 = 1][ * 2 + 1 = 3][*2 = 6][*2 = 12][*2 = 24][ * 2 + 1 = 49][*2 = 98][end of the command line]

TLSS
will print the stack parceque:
[=>][I/O][=>][output the character at the top of the stack]
[=>] mean that they need the next command to work because it will not work alone, the next command is the result of the two command together
